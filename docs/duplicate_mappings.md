# Self-Assessment Duplicate Mappings

The following questions were identified as mapping to the exact same Readiness Sub-Parameter within the same Project. When the import script ran, a student's scores for these overlapping questions were mathematically averaged together into a single master score for that parameter.

### Project: Accounts
**Domain:** Operational Readiness ➔ **Parameter:** Process & Project Management

**Questions Averaged Together:**
- I identified accounting entries and recorded them with accuracy.
- I followed a structured process to complete accounting tasks.

---

### Project: SDP
**Domain:** Professional Readiness ➔ **Parameter:** Professional Conduct & Ethics

**Questions Averaged Together:**
- I understood the agreement we signed with the business and took responsibility for delivering what we committed to.
- I behaved professionally, respected commitments, and took responsibility for my actions while working with the business owner.

---

### Project: SDP
**Domain:** Entrepreneurial Readiness ➔ **Parameter:** Sales & Outreach

**Questions Averaged Together:**
- I confidently engaged with the business owner, handled questions or objections, and communicated the value of our work professionally.
- I pitched our work clearly and confidently, engaged the audience with relevant questions, and built a professional connection with the business owner.

---

### Project: SDP
**Domain:** Innovation Readiness ➔ **Parameter:** Customer-Centered Insights

**Questions Averaged Together:**
- I was able to identify a real service problem or opportunity by observing customers and interviewing stakeholders, not by making assumptions.
- When plans didn’t work as expected, I helped the team adapt, solve problems, and keep the project moving forward.

---

### Project: SDP
**Domain:** Innovation Readiness ➔ **Parameter:** Prototyping & Agile Development

**Questions Averaged Together:**
- I tested ideas quickly, used feedback to improve them, and made decisions based on what worked rather than what I personally liked.
- I actively contributed to building, testing, and improving our prototype based on real feedback from users or the business owner.
